The Sourcer

Problem Space

Top-of-funnel sourcing is the most time-intensive and repetitive part of recruiting, draining strategic recruiter time and slowing time-to-fill.

Solution

An autonomous candidate discovery and profile enrichment agent that surfaces pre-qualified candidates from LinkedIn, Google Jobs, and talent databases without manual sourcing.

Impact

Removed manual sourcing from the recruiter workflow, letting the team handle higher req volume without added headcount and contributing to a 125% productivity gain.

×

The Caller

Problem Space

Initial outreach, screening, and compensation/notice-period negotiation are high-volume conversations that bottleneck recruiters and delay scheduling.

Solution

An AI-driven engagement agent that pitches roles, screens candidates, negotiates compensation and notice periods, and schedules interviews across WhatsApp, email, and voice.

Impact

Compressed the engage-to-schedule cycle and kept candidate pipelines warm at scale, improving conversion and reducing drop-off between sourcing and interview.

×

The Validator

Problem Space

Unstructured screening produces inconsistent shortlists, weak client submissions, and avoidable rejections at the client-review stage.

Solution

A structured evaluation engine that scores candidates against the job description, flags gaps, and produces a ranked, recruiter-ready shortlist for client submission.

Impact

Raised submission quality and shortlist acceptance through consistent, evidence-based scoring — turning screening discipline into measurably higher client acceptance.
