Recruiter’s Diary

Problem Space

Recruiters often rely on scattered, manual spreadsheets to track roles, submissions, and pipeline flow, leading to data fragmentation and inefficiency.

Solution

An AI-enabled workflow hub that replaces manual spreadsheets. It enables quick capture of pipeline updates and tracks lifecycle metrics in a centralized dashboard featuring a built-in conversational analytics agent.

Impact

Standardized pipeline reporting, drastically reduced administrative overhead for recruiters, and provided leadership with instant, conversational visibility into hiring velocity and bottlenecks.

×

EBES (Employee Best Effort Score)

Problem Space

Evaluating recruiter performance purely based on hires often ignores the upstream effort and quality of daily activities, making it difficult to coach or predict long-term success.

Solution

An AI-assisted framework that evaluates individual effort, performance signals, and activity patterns across the entire hiring workflow, outputting a structured behavioral and effort scoring system.

Impact

Shifted performance management from reactive (hire count) to proactive (effort quality). Enhanced leadership's ability to identify training needs early and recognize consistent high-effort contributors before placements occurred.

Automater

Problem Space

Top-of-funnel candidate sourcing and follow-up communications are highly repetitive tasks that drain strategic recruiter time and slow down time-to-fill metrics.

Solution

An AI-powered automation toolkit designed specifically for talent acquisition workflows. It automates initial sourcing outreach, data tracking, and interview coordination logic.

Impact

Significantly automated repetitive top-of-funnel activities, allowing the recruitment team to handle higher req volume without increasing headcount, and driving a 125% increase in recruiter productivity metrics over time.
